Overview

REPresentation Alignment (REPA) makes diffusion transformer training significantly easier. Our framework, REPA, explicitly aligns the diffusion model representation with powerful pretrained visual representation through a simple regularization. Notably, model training becomes significantly more efficient and effective, and achieves >17.5x faster convergence than the vanilla model. In terms of final generation quality, our approach achieves state-of-the-art results of FID=1.42 using classifier-free guidance with the guidance interval.
